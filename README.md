# SomaShare

PROJECT DESCRIPTION:
SomaShare is an online textbook exchange platform, a secure platform, for students to exchange textbooks, by adding student identity protocols and localized payment solutions that will protect both parties, to reduce fraud. The platform includes an implementation of user reviews and authentication to ensure an extra layer of safety and security. SomaShare will provide students with affordable access to textbooks across African campuses. Additionally, it provides administrative supervision to maintain the platform's integrity, creates a peer-to-peer marketplace to help enforce a user-friendly digital platform.
It has been specially designed to enhance resource circulation and sustainability to promote an economy within campus communities, ensuring that textbooks remain in active use across multiple academic institutions. The platform's main objective is to improve academic equity and performance seeing as many, if not most, higher education institutions do not provide students with free learning materials, making it hard for them to get the necessary textbooks, and this threatens the academic potential of millions of students.
